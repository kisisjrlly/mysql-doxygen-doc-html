build PBKDF2 from a MCF notation.

  • ${prefix}${rounds}${salt}${checksum}
  • ${prefix}${rounds}${salt}

rounds : [1-9][0-9]*

salt : [^$]*

checksum : [./a-zA-Z0-0]*

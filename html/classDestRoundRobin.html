◆ cleanup_quarantine()

void DestRoundRobin::cleanup_quarantine ( )
protectedvirtualnoexcept

Checks and removes servers from quarantine.

This method removes servers from quarantine while trying to establish a connection. It is used in a seperate thread and will update the quarantine list, and will keep trying until the list is empty. A conditional variable is used to notify the thread servers were quarantined.
